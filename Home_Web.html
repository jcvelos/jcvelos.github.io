How to Navigate


1. Hover over home and click it if you want to see the index or the main home where you can see the assignment in ITC.

2. Hover over to the ITC Assignment Nav Bar and choose if you want to see the assignments.

3. After you hover to the ITC assignments Nav Bar, you will see two options which are the Converter and the Calculator.

4. Under the Converter is the Length and Width converter, while under the Calculator is the Factorial, Income Tax Calculator, and the Simple Payroll.

5. Hover on to the options and you will see the links of the assignments. Click to choose which one of them would you like to visit then boom you can access the assignments tasked by our ITC Professor, John Roy Geralde.

Note: There is music on each link you visit, so I highly recommend lowering your PC volume so that it will not be an inconvenience. Thank you for looking at my website and have fun!
